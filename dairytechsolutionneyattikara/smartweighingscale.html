  • Smart Weighing Scale

Smart Weighing System is a geo-tagged weighing system created specially to address the issues prevalent in cattle rearing. With our Smart Weighing Scale, cattle's daily milk production can be recorded accurately, which is, in turn, linked to a central database. This will also allow the farmers and breeders to better pinpoint the locations of their livestock and help them maintain a better herd.

Benefits

  • Ensures accurate weight capture
  • Includes geolocation and a timestamp
  • Recording the authenticity of milk
  • Milk quantity is recorded automatically without manual intervention
