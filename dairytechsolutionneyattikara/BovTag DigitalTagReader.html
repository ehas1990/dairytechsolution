  • BovTag – Digital Tag Reader

BovTag is an Android-based application that uses the camera of the Smartphone or Tablet to capture the image of the existing ear tag and extracts a unique tag identification number. Our Digital tag reader is designed to make identifying livestock and tracking their history easier. The app then records the date, time and GPS coordinates of where the data was collected. This device saves a lot of time and cost in the process of animal identification and reduces human errors. It is a quick, mobile, and reliable process of identifying and tracking livestock. With the BovTag app, you can create a fully digital inventory of your farm and data collection is available at the click of a button.

Benefits

  • It enables cattle identification to be easy, effective & efficient
  • Enhances transparency between all stakeholders
  • Error-free and more accurate reading of existing ear tags.
  • Featured as an affordable alternative to other expensive tag readers
  • Data is authenticated through geotagging.
  • Integrates with any farm management software or animal management application

Key Feature

  • It can be used online and offline.
  • Tags are read in less than ten seconds
  • Ensures better image quality with auto-adjustment
  • A sophisticated image processing platform based on complex algorithms
  • Even scratched or dirty ear tags can be read, as well as those of any size, colour, or font.
  • Date, time, and location of the data collection
  • It is possible to read tags from a distance of 1.2 metres (4 feet) using the built-in zoom camera of a mobile phone.
  • Low lighting (using a mobile phone flash), varied camera angles, and misaligned tags will not affect the tag's readability.
