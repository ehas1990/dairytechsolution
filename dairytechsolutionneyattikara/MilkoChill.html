  • MilkoChill-Instant Milk Chiller

MilkoChill Instant milk chilling system ensures the purity of the milk is preserved at the farmer's doorstep. Our innovative and affordable instant milk chiller makes it possible to chill milk at the source to provide a better return to the farmer. The system provides for instant cooling of milk even in the absence of electricity to ensure the quality of milk is retained at the source and bacterial growth is minimized. It has been designed by keeping in mind the requirements of both urban and rural areas. It ensures there is no loss of quality, purity or taste of the milk at the farmer's doorstep which helps them to maximize their incomes by reducing spoilage and increasing shelf life. MilkoChill can chill 250 litres of milk in one hour.

Instant Milk Chilling based on Thermodynamic design

Imagine being able to chill any type of milk in three minutes! MilkoChill is the world's most advanced and patented instant milk chilling system. Only MilkoChill is based on a unique thermo-dynamic design for fast and perfect results.

Low energy consumption

The MilkoChill Instant Milk Chiller from Milkonator is an innovative way to cool milk is designed to cool milk as close to ambient temperature as possible in the shortest time. The result is lower energy consumption and less maintenance compared to traditional refrigeration systems.

Hot water generation

MilkoChill provides up to 32 litres of 100°C to 120°C (212°F) hot water per hour at low power consumption. Ideal for home, canteens and activities centres.

Benefits

  • Chill 250 litres of milk in one hour.
  • Generates 100 Litres of hot water at 55°C
  • Low energy consumption (1000W)
