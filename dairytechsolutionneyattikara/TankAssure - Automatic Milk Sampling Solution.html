  • TankAssure - Automatic Milk Sampling Solution

The accurate data of milk quantity and quality collected by milk tankers from various milk collection points on different routes is an essential piece of information in the milk value chain. Even minor deviations from the expected milk quantity and quality may hinder the effectiveness and profitability of the entire chain of the milk collection process. Dairytech solutions have leveraged the RFID and GPRS technology and developed a solution for automatic milk sampling, that enables the tanker drivers to collect samples and send them to a central laboratory using SMS, which facilitates prompt and accurate data analysis of each tankers' yield.

Benefits

  • Milk quantities transferred in the tanker and the temperature of milk at the DCS location will be shown in real-time - introducing transparency between the collection point and the dairy
  • Using this method, data is automatically recorded, thus reducing the possibility of human error.
  • Milk samples taken by auto sampling are usually accurate, so farmers can be confident they will receive correct payment for their milk.
  • Data is recorded automatically and digitally and is stored directly on the server.
  • By automating the sampling process, sampling errors are greatly reduced, and samples are then more accurate
  • Dairy will become more effective and profitable with accurate sampling and quantity of milk
  • Power is on or off for multiple compressors and generators
  • Analyzes quantity, temperature, the agitator for mixing, the compressor, and the CIP system for cleaning in the process.

Today, Diary Tech solutions offers innovative equipment and software solutions to ensure that the purity of milk is retained at every step
